And Welcome...
We are Saints Sled Dog Rescue, a non-profitable charity dedicated to rescuing and rehoming Siberian Husky and Alaskan Malamute breeds.

We were founded by Heather Saville in January 2013 and rescue on average 120 dogs per year!
