HELLO FRIENDS!

We are Saints Sled Dog Rescue, a non-profit charity dedicated to rescuing and rehoming Siberian Huskies and Alaskan Malamutes.

Founded by Heather Saville in 2013, we rescue over 120 dogs every year.
